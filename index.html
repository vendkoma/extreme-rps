History of Rock, Paper, Scissors

According to historians, RPS may be the first game ever invented by man. Evidence has been found to suggest that neanderthals, inspired by the discovery that a sharp stick could be used to remove the material hiding their friends' baubles, played a game known as "stone, tree bark, pointy stick". Below, we will delve into many such theories regarding the origin and timeline of our great game.
